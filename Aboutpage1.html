Paddy Thresher Machine

Paddy thresher machine is used to quickly separate rice grains from the harvested paddy stalks. Instead of beating the bundles manually, farmers feed the paddy into the machine, where a rotating drum hits the crop and loosens the grains. A fan or blower then removes the light chaff and dust, making the grains cleaner.

This machine helps farmers save a lot of time and effort compared to traditional threshing by hand. It increases productivity, reduces labor costs, and ensures that more grains are collected without damage. The video shows how easy it is to operate the machine in the field and highlights how useful it is during the rice harvesting season.

The agricultural water pump machine is used to supply water for irrigation in farms. It lifts water from sources such as wells, borewells, rivers, or ponds and delivers it to the fields through pipes. The pump works with the help of an electric motor or diesel engine that creates suction and pushes water to the required area efficiently.

This machine helps farmers provide regular and sufficient water to crops, especially during dry seasons. It saves time and labor while improving crop growth and productivity. With proper installation and regular maintenance, the water pump ensures smooth operation and long service life.
